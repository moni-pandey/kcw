FAQ

Q

What is the big idea behind KCW?

A

KCW is a way for the local community to support local artists by helping them with major expenses like rent, food and gear. It can help budding artists avoid gentrification from cities becoming extremely expensive, and it can help patrons support their local art scene.

Q

Do I get something from the artists for supporting them?

A

Currently No. KCW is currently only a platform for artists to showcase their art and work in progress and seek support from the local community. The artists are not expected to sell/give any of their art. The artists, however, are free to give their art to patrons out of the platform's purview.

Q

What platforms is KCW available on?

A

Version 1.0 of KCW can be found on Android and iOS app stores. As the community grows, we will also bring it to the web.

Q

Who can be an artist on KCW?

A

Anyone can sign up to be an artist on KCW. Artists can also share updates on the art they are creating so their local supporters can know what they are doing and support the artists they like.

Q

What kind of art can I showcase at KCW?

A

You can showcase any kind of art you would like on KCW. Since it is a mobile app, it will be easiest to showcase visual arts like paintings, sculptures, vases, chefs, etc.

Q

How you do you that the artist is real? Or that they are using their funds appropriately?

A

KCW is aimed to help local communities support their artists. The expectation is that community can also validate the artist offline if they are suspicious. Currently, KCW does not have any validation mechanisms in place. We might include mechanisms in future version if we see this being a major problem on the platform.

Q

Who can be a patron on KCW?

A

Anyone can be a patron on KCW. You can make contributions of any size. Our minimum recommended contribution is 10$. This is to avoid processing fees taking up a lot of contribution. You can support an artist by making a one-time or monthly recurring payments.
